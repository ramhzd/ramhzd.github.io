FTC Robotics Experience 2: Teleoperated Game Challenge Description: In this project, I focused on teleoperated robot control during an FTC competition. Using Java and gamepad input, I wrote code to control the robot's movements, manipulate game objects, and interact with other robots. The challenge required precise motor control, strategic decision-making, and real-time responsiveness to opponents' actions.

Reflection: Controlling the robot during live competition was an adrenaline rush. I had to stay calm under pressure, communicate effectively with my alliance partners, and adapt to changing game conditions. This experience improved my ability to think on my feet and reinforced the importance of teamwork in robotics. It was a great opportunity to apply programming skills in a competitive environment and learn how software and hardware work together in real-time.
