Teaching Philosophy

My Philosophy
My teaching philosophy aligns perfectly with the principles upheld by my learn website. Experiential learning is at the core of my approach, and this platform embodies that ethos by immersing students in the world of FTC robotics. The fusion of theory and practice is paramount, and my learning website provides an excellent avenue for students to apply their programming knowledge in a real-world context.
FTC robotics, as exemplified by my experiences, serves as a powerful gateway to complex programming concepts. Integrating sensors, writing autonomous navigation code, and mastering teleoperated control not only tested our technical prowess but also honed our problem-solving skills.
It underscored the transformative potential of robotics education. Through the platform's immersive challenges, students can embark on a journey that not only enhances their practical programming skills but also encourages a broader perspective on engineering, teamwork, and innovation. My learning website plays a pivotal role in fostering this learning journey. By providing resources, guidance, and a community of like-minded learners, it empowers students to dive into the exciting world of robotics programming. The platform bridges the gap between theory and practice, enabling students to see the immediate application of their coding efforts.
It encourages a growth mindset, where challenges are seen as opportunities for learning and experimentation.
